# CHBH Hackathon 2026


The CHBH is proud to host the second Hackathon! a collaborative, non‑competitive event focused on learning and network‑building.

We're bringing participants together to explore project, share coding skills, and learn new computing techniques. You can either

- Join an existing project where someone will guide participants through new tools and approaches
- Propose your own project that others can join or
- Bring along a specific piece of work that you and your collaborators want to focus on for a few days.

We will come together as a group for keynote talks and project check-ins throughout the two days.

The atmosphere will be inclusive, approachable and curious.
