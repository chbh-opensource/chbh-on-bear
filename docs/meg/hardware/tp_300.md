# NATUS TP-300 (Etymotic ER-2)

**[MEG Audio Connections](../../meg/pdfs/MEG_Audio_Connections.pdf)**

The **[TIP-300](../../meg/pdfs/tip-300.pdf)**, **also known as Etymotic ER-2**, is a **high-quality Tubephone Insert Earphone** provided by MEGIN.<br />
It **connects to the "Patient" socket on the gantry sidepanel**, and the **<span style="font-size:large">&frac14;</span> plugs** on the **Control Room desk** are attached, **via adapters, to the Stim PC sound**.

![NATUS TIP-300](../../images/meg/natus-tip-300.png){width=45% align=right}

<br />
Due to the **high audio quality and fidelity of the sound received**, this system is the **preferred choice in most MEG studies**.
However, the **transducers do produce a significant magnetic field** that can **be detected by the MEG sensors**.<br />
It is important to **securely fix the transducers** at the back, or side, of the gantry chair, so they **don't move during an acquisition session**.

**Two sizes of eartip are available; Yellow (*13mm*) and Beige (*10mm*).**

!!! warning "NATUS and SOUNDPixx eartips are NOT TRANSFERABLE between systems. The tube diameter for each is different."

**[How to insert the foam eartips](../../images/meg/NATUS_Eartip_usage.png)**
<br />
!!! Info "It is reccommended to separate the red and blue tubing at the midle white connector, to allow easy insertion of the eartip into the participant's ear."

!!! warning "DO NOT throw away the white connector, when disposing of used eartips!"
