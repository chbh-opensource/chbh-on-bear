# Ishihara Colour Vision Deficiency Test

The CHBH MEG Lab has the 14-plate **Ishihara Test** for Colour Deficiency "*Colour Blindness*".

The current **passing score** is **12 correct of the 14 red/green test plates** (*not including the demonstration plate*) indicating normal colour vision. <br />
Any score **below 12** indicates **colour vision deficiency**.

Unfortunately the **type of red-green deficiency** ("**protan**" or "**deutan**") in Participants **cannot be characterised as those test plates are not available** in the book.
