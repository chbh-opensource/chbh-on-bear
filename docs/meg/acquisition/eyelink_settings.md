# EyeLink Settings

**<span style=font-size:medium;color:maroon>Screen Dimensions:</span>**

- **Width: 830mm** - <span style="color:blue">**Width** of the **viewable portion of the projected Stim PC monitor image**</span>.
- **Height: 470mm** - <span style="color:blue">**Height** of the **viewable portion of the projected Stim PC monitor image**</span>.

!!! Note "<span style=font-size:medium;color:red>NOTE:</span> These values may change, depending on the position of the projector screen relative to the projector mirror.<br />Stimulus paradigm will also determine the screen position - use tape on the MSR floor to help with projector screen placement."


**<span style=font-size:medium;color:maroon>Display Resolution of Stim PC monitor:</span>**

- **Width: 1920 pixels**
- **Height: 1080 pixels**

!!! info "This setting only needs to be changed, in FINAL.INI, if a different display resolution is used to the one above."


**<span style=font-size:medium;color:maroon>Eye-to-Screen Distance:</span>**

- **1000mm** - <span style="color:blue">**Distance** from **the eye** to the top of the **viewable portion of the projected Stim PC monitor image**</span>.
- **1050mm** - <span style="color:blue">**Distance** from **the eye** to the bottom of the **viewable portion of the projected Stim PC monitor image**</span>).

!!! Note "<span style=font-size:medium;color:red>NOTE:</span> These values may change, depending on the position of the projector screen relative to the gantry chair/participant. e.g. 1500mm.<br />Some Operators also measure *Distance from the eye to the <u>center</u> of the viewable portion of the projected Stim PC monitor image.*"


**<span style=font-size:medium;color:maroon>EyeLink lens defualt setup:</span>**

- **35mmm - <span style="color:blue">Used by most Operators. 50mm lens is available on the MSR shelving if required.</span>**
	- **Swap back to 35mm after use.**
- "<span style="color:green">*I put the eye tracker very close to the participant, so smaller lens will give me a wider angle to cover the whole visual fields*</span>" **Yali Pan**.


**<span style=font-size:medium;color:maroon>Camera-to-Screen Distance:</span>**

- **150mm - <span style="color:blue">Only applicable in "Remote" mode (which is not used), so can safely be ignored.</span>**
