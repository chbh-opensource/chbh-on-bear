# Becoming a MEG Operator

**<span style="color:blue">Minimum academic qualification for MEG Operator training/solo MEG acquiring:</span>**

- **<span style="color:maroon">PhD Student, but also RA (any academic level) hired for the project.</span>**
- **<span style="color:blue">After discussion, now allowing <span style="color:maroon">MSc Students</span> to undergo training to provide help to PostDocs/PhDs. However, like with *some* RAs...</span>**
	- **<span style="color:blue">They may not be signed off as official MEG Operators.</span>**
	- **<span style="color:blue">They won't be allowed to enter/use the MEG unsupervised.</span>**

To **request initial MEG training, please email:** <br />

- **<span style="color:maroon">chbh-help[at]contacts.bham.ac.uk</span>** with the Subject line "**MEG Operator training request**". <br />

!!! Note "<span style="font-size:medium">CHBH Building Induction will be required first, before placing the above email.</span>"

**Anyone new to CHBH**, needing swipe access etc., is **required to complete the *CHBH Canvas Building Induction Training***.<br />
Head over to **[https://canvas.bham.ac.uk/enroll/4H8YET](https://canvas.bham.ac.uk/enroll/4H8YET)** and select **"CHBH Building Induction Training"**.

!!! Note "<span style="font-size:medium">If any issues are encountered with the above, please email <span style="color:blue">chbh[at]contacts.bham.ac.uk</span></span>"

**Once Induction training is complete**, a MEG Operator training session will be arranged (as requested above), ideally for a small group of 2-3 trainees at a time. <br />

!!! Note "<span style="font-size:medium">Individual training is a possibility, but maximising Acquiring time is paramount so this option may not always be available.</span>"

**A full day** is now required for initial training, so ***be aware of that when making a request***.

Once complete, **(*at least*) three Acquiring Data sessions** will **need to be shadowed with an accomplished MEG Operator**, who will **need to be happy with the MEG knowledge learnt** (by helping them out/asking them relevant questions etc).

The **MEG Operator Checklist, gone over during training**, will then need to be **[downloaded locally and filled in/digitally signed](../../meg/pdfs/meg_operator_checklist_v4-0c.pdf)**, before **having the form digitally signed/dated by a shadowed MEG Operator** and then **finally digitally signed/dated by MEG Support**.

!!! Info "<span style="font-size:medium">If required, [Completing the MEG Operator Checklist correctly](../acquisition/checklistfillin.md).</span>"

The **CHBH personnel database** will then **be updated** to show "***MEG Opr.***" as being checked, and **addition** to **<span style="color:maroon">Chbh-meg-operator[at]lists.bham.ac.uk</span>** will **allow receiving and sending of emails concerning the MEG**.

In order to then **make** **[MEG bookings](../acquisition/megbookings.md)**, a **MEG project proposal will need to have been approved**, and a **Calpendo** project code subsequently **created to book against**.

**A Calpendo account will be required**, as will **being added to the relevant Calpendo project or projects**.<br /> 
To **request a Calpendo account**, again **email** **<span style="color:blue">chbh[at]contacts.bham.ac.uk</span>**
