=== **<span style="font-size:medium;color:blue">MEG BOOKING RULES:</span>** ===

* **All bookings are requested via Calpendo**. 
** **''Please read, and carefully check, your emails, and re-check Calpendo (once a booking has been requested) for any updates.**'' 
** **''The booking has to be approved by the MEG Support Officer, or one of the Administrators if he is away, before it is final.**'' 
** **''Bookings made within normal working hours that conform to the pre-defined slots will normally be approved within a (working) day.**''




* **Due to the reduced liquefaction time (Monday to Wednesday)** it's unlikely you'll be able to acquire data outside these hours. It may be possible to extend acquiring by 30min after the Afternoon sessions (Thursday to Saturday) but this does depend on prior usage/Helium tank pressure. Check the Scheduler.
** **If you're unsure** if you can 'extend' your Thursday to Saturday Afternoon slot, contact MEG Support.
** **Any questions regarding your MEG bookings** , contact MEG Support. <br />
*** **<span style="color:blue">j.winter@bham.ac.uk</span>**


* **<span style="color:maroon">Please be aware that if you make booking requests, and/or use the Lab, <span style="color:red">during the weekend (Saturdays ONLY for Lab usage)</span> or make bookings/use the Lab when <span style="color:red">MEG Support is on AL</span> you may not get an immediate response to any booking approvals or support requests.</span>**


* ** If you are Acquiring data on a Saturday, a Testing Buddy is required.**
::- ** Buddies need to be present for experiment set-up, and remain ‘on call’ within the building for the entirety of the Acquiring session.**
::- ** A Buddy must hold at least ‘General User’ status in the CHBH, or if they intend to help with experiments, then they must be trained up as MEG General User or be a full MEG Operator.**

::- **<span style="color:maroon">A Buddy may not be necessary if you're just testing equipment (stimuli/triggers etc) out of hours.</span>**


**<span style="color:red">No MEG Lab usage, of any kind, is permitted on Sundays!</span>**.



* ** You will need to have requested out-of-hours access (click [https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=z8oksN7eQUKhXDyX1VPp8yoGKIcHP8FHh82NAR1nFAJUQ01ISTJHSUQ0U09GUEdTMEs0T0paT0tXMiQlQCN0PWcu HERE]) and read the [[Weekend Working]] protocol before you can make an out-of-hours, e.g. Saturday, booking request.**


* **If you book the MEG for a shorter or longer period** than the pre-defined 2 hours, please make sure not to create any unnecessary gaps that are difficult to be filled.


* **A Project code is required to make any bookings** (you will receive a project code once your project application and project proposal presentation are approved).


* **You cannot use more than the total hours assigned to your project code (this holds for testing software/equipment, pilot hours as well as data acquiring).** If you need more hours please arrange this with your PI and ask for approval from the Management Team and MEG lead. (**<span style="color:blue">H.park@bham.ac.uk</span>**). Extra hours will then be added as "Unfunded" hours.


* **Users are allowed to book up to 6 hours (3 slots) per Project per Week**.<br />After all Operators have **had a chance to make bookings**, you are **allowed to book two more slots (4 hours), Monday to Friday**, if there are free slots available.<br /> Please send a courtesy email to **<span style="color:blue">"Chbh-meg-operator@lists.bham.ac.uk"</span>** asking that you'd like to make an extra booking and is everyone OK with that, **BEFORE** you make your extra booking.<br />
** An **extra slot booked on a Saturday** would **not count** towards the allowance, meaning up to **12 hours (or 6 slots) a week could be booked** (''if other Operators are agreeable'').

* **<span style="color:red">NOTE: </span>** If not all Operators have booked their six hours, you may be asked to relinquish any extra bookings you have made, above your normally allowed hours.


* **Bookings made for testing scripts etc. out of hours** (when the MEG is in liquefaction) **will not be counted** towards the weekly allowance.


* **Bookings** can be made up to 3 months ahead.

=== **<span style="font-size:medium;color:blue">MEG BOOKING CANCELLATION POLICY</span>** ===

* **72 hours’ notice is required** to cancel a booking.

* **Only weekdays (Monday–Friday)** are counted when calculating the 72 hours.
** **Weekends do NOT count** toward the 72-hour period.

* **Hours are refunded** if they are **cancelled 72 hours beforehand (not including weekends)**.

* If a scan is cancelled within 72 working hours **and the slot is not rebooked by another MEG Operator**, the project will be charged **the full cost** of the session. **<span style="color:red">@</span>** 
**  If unfortunately a **Participant cancels**, please email **<span style="color:maroon">"Chbh-meg-operator@lists.bham.ac.uk"</span>** informing that your  booked slot is now free. 
*** You will need to **delete your booking ''before'' sending the email**. <br />There's always the possibility **another Operator could then use the slot themselves at the last minute - and your hours would then be recoverable.** 

* For every other situation, hours are not refunded, and Operators can request additional unfunded hours to the Directors via the Operations Manager. 

* The **MEG Support Officer/Administrators reserve the right to cancel bookings in case of technical problems/Service or Maintenance requirements. **
** **If bookings are cancelled because of these issues, they will not be charged for.**
