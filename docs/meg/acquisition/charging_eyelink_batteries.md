# Charging EyeLink batteries


## Original

Press **TEST** button to check the battery power level. If charging required ...

- Plug in **correct** battery charge lead (**#1** for **#1** etc).
- **<span style="color:green">GREEN</span>** *Power* LED will light, also **<span style="color:orange">ORANGE</span>** *Charge* LED.
- *Charge* LED will **go out** when battery is fully charged / "**Complete**".

!!! Note
    Make sure the **battery is switched OFF when charging**.<br />
    **Unplug** the battery once fully charged. **Do not** leave attached to the charger.<br />
    **Do not** leave a battery on charge overnight.<br />
		- <span style="color:blue">**Add a piece of *Micropore* tape to the battery front, to indicate the battery needs charging next day.</span>**

- **Use a fully-charged battery** for an acquisition session (if possible).
- **Swap to a new battery** between acquisitions. 
- **Put** first battery **on charge**.

!!! info "**<span style="color:maroon">"A battery with 2-3 hours left should be fine for a short experiment. Do not risk using it for a 2-hour experiment."</span> (*SR Research*).**"

## New

**[LRBB User Manual](../../meg/pdfs/LRBB_User_Manual_2023_06_02_Rev1_1.pdf)**

To **check** the current battery level, **move** the Battery POWER Switch to the **ON position**, and **press** the **Battery level TEST** button.

![New Battery Frontpanel](../../images/meg/New_Battery_Front.jpg){width=40% align=left}

- There are **five LEDs** in the **Battery Level Indicator** window on top of the battery (**<span style="color:green">3 GREEN</span>**, **<span style="color:orange">1 ORANGE</span>**, and **<span style="color:red">1 red</span>)**. 
- **Each LED** represents approximately **20% of the battery charge**.
- *The Indicator is disabled during charging and when the POWER switch is OFF to prevent accidental discharge*.

<br />

|  LED State <br />(<span style="color:blue">With Power Switch ON <br />and Battery Level button pressed</span>)  |  Charge State  |  Action to take  |
|  ------- |  ------ |  ------- |
|  All LEDs are lit (**<span style="color:green">3 GREEN</span>** plus<br />**<span style="color:red">RED</span>** and **<span style="color:orange">ORANGE</span>**)   | Fully charged   | Ready for extended use<br />or storage |
|  **<span style="color:green">1 GREEN</span>** LED (plus **<span style="color:red">RED</span>** and <br />**<span style="color:orange">ORANGE</span>**)  | Less than 50% charged  | Please charge |
|  Only **<span style="color:orange">ORANGE</span>** and **<span style="color:red">RED</span>** LEDs  | Battery Level low, <br />limited time remaining  | Please charge before<br>starting further use |
|  **<span style="color:red">RED</span>** LED only  | Critically low, may lose<br />power in a few minutes | Please charge as<br />soon as possible |
|  **No LEDs lit** | Battery completely<br />discharged | Please charge<br />immediately! |


![Battery Level Indicator](../../images/meg/New_Battery_Level_Indicator.jpg)

<br />
**<span style="font-size:large">To Charge</span>**

- **Plug in** the **labelled charger cable** (e.g. **#4**) into the **appropriate** battery.
- **Switch on** the **labelled** (e.g. **#4**) **Mains plug**. The **LEDs** on the charger **will display its status**. 

| LED | Status |
| ------- |  ------ |
|  **<span style="color:RED">RED</span>**  | Charging is in progress |
|  **<span style="color:#FDDA0D">YELLOW</span>**  | The battery is fully charged and is now in trickle charging mode<br />The Mains plug can be switched off, and the charger lead disconnected from the battery |
|  **<span style="color:green">GREEN</span>**  | Trickle charging is complete  |

**<span style="color:blue">Typical charging time is ~4 hours (from the Battery Level showing a RED-only LED)</span>.**

!!! Note
    Make sure the **battery is switched OFF when charging**.<br />
    **Unplug** the battery once fully charged. **Do not** leave attached to the charger.<br />
    **Do not** leave a battery on charge overnight.<br />
		- <span style="color:blue">**Add a piece of *Micropore* tape to the battery front, to indicate the battery needs charging next day.</span>**

- **Use a fully-charged battery** for an acquisition session (if possible).
- **Swap to a new battery** between acquisitions. 
- **Put** first battery **on charge**.

!!! info "**<span style="color:maroon">"A battery with 2-3 hours left should be fine for a short experiment. Do not risk using it for a 2-hour experiment."</span> (*SR Research*).**"
